DSP: moving average calculation

1. Sine

Sine wave generated by doing Math.sin(sampleNumber / (sampleFreq / (Math.PI * 2))).

2. Sine with noise

Noise generated by adding a random value to the amplitude of every sample.

3. Moving average calculation on sine with noise

Calculated by moving through the samples, looking back for half the samplesize and looking forward for the other half.
